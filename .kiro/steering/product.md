# Suryamas ERP - Product Overview

Suryamas is a comprehensive enterprise resource planning (ERP) system designed for retail and food production businesses. The system manages:

- **Multi-branch operations** with company-wide and branch-specific contexts
- **Inventory management** including goods receipts, processing, stock movements, and warehouse operations
- **Purchase management** from requests through orders to invoice processing
- **Accounting** with chart of accounts, journals, fiscal periods, and financial reporting
- **Food production** with menus, recipes, WIP tracking, and COGS calculation
- **POS integration** with sync, aggregation, and reconciliation workflows
- **Bank reconciliation** with statement imports and settlement grouping
- **HR and payroll** with employee management and branch assignments
- **Payment processing** with multiple methods, alerting, and cash flow tracking

The system follows a permission-based access control model where users require explicit permissions to access modules and perform actions.
